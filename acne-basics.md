# Understanding Acne: The Basics

Acne is a complex skin condition that affects millions of people worldwide. It occurs when hair follicles become plugged with oil and dead skin cells.

## Types of Acne

1.  **Comedonal Acne:** Includes whiteheads and blackheads.
2.  **Inflammatory Acne:** Includes papules and pustules.
3.  **Cystic Acne:** Deep, painful lumps under the skin.

## Common Causes

-   **Hormonal Changes:** Often seen during puberty or menstrual cycles.
-   **Excess Oil Production:** Overactive sebaceous glands.
-   **Bacteria:** *Cutibacterium acnes* (C. acnes) growth.
-   **Inflammation:** The body's immune response to clogged pores.
