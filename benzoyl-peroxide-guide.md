# Benzoyl Peroxide: The Bacteria Fighter

Benzoyl Peroxide (BPO) is one of the most effective over-the-counter and prescription treatments for inflammatory acne.

## How It Works

BPO works by introducing oxygen into the pores, which kills *C. acnes* bacteria (which cannot survive in oxygen-rich environments). It also helps to unclog pores and reduce inflammation.

## Key Benefits

-   **Anti-bacterial:** No known bacterial resistance.
-   **Keratolytic:** Helps shed dead skin cells.
-   **Anti-inflammatory:** Reduces redness of papules and pustules.

## Usage Tips

-   **Contact Time:** Some prefer "short contact therapy" (leaving it on for 5-10 mins then washing off) to reduce irritation.
-   **Staining:** BPO can bleach towels and pillowcases. Use white linens or ensure the product is fully dry.
-   **Combination Therapy:** Often paired with antibiotics for better results. For example, [Peroclin 2.5 Gel](https://pillvibe.com/product-detail/peroclin-2-5-gel) combines Clindamycin with BPO to target both bacteria and inflammation effectively.

## Precautions

-   Can be drying; always follow with a good moisturizer.
-   May cause initial redness or peeling.
