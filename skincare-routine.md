# Step-by-Step Skincare Routines

A consistent routine is the foundation of clear skin. Here are two sample routines based on common acne treatment plans.

## Routine 1: The "Gold Standard" (Retinoid Focus)

This routine is designed for those using potent retinoids like [Tretizen 20mg](https://pillvibe.com/product-detail/tretizen-20mg) (oral) or topical [Macret 0.1 Gel](https://pillvibe.com/product-detail/macret-0-1-gel-20g).

### Morning (AM)
1.  **Gentle Cleanser:** Remove overnight oils without stripping the skin.
2.  **Hydrating Toner/Serum:** (Optional) Focus on hydration.
3.  **Moisturizer:** Lock in moisture.
4.  **Sunscreen (SPF 30+):** Absolute must.

### Evening (PM)
1.  **Cleanser:** Remove dirt and sunscreen.
2.  **Moisturizer:** Apply to damp skin.
3.  **Wait 20 Minutes:** Ensure skin is completely dry.
4.  **Retinoid:** Apply a pea-sized amount. [Tazret Forte](https://pillvibe.com/product-detail/tazret-forte-cream-for-healthy-skin) is applied at this stage.
5.  **Moisturizer (Again):** The "Sandwich Method" for sensitive skin.

## Routine 2: The "Inflammation Buster" (Azelaic Acid Focus)

Best for those with sensitive, acne-prone skin or rosacea.

### Morning (AM)
1.  **Cleanser:** Gentle wash.
2.  **Azelaic Acid:** Apply [Ezanic Cream 20](https://pillvibe.com/product-detail/ezanic-cream-20) to dry skin.
3.  **Moisturizer:** Soothe the skin.
4.  **Sunscreen:** Protect from UV.

### Evening (PM)
1.  **Cleanser:** Thorough clean.
2.  **Treatment:** [Peroclin 2.5 Gel](https://pillvibe.com/product-detail/peroclin-2-5-gel) on active breakouts.
3.  **Moisturizer:** Barrier repair.
