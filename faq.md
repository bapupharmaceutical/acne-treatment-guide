# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Q: How long does it take to see results?
**A:** Most acne treatments take 8 to 12 weeks of consistent use. You may experience a "purging" phase during the first few weeks with retinoids.

## Q: Can I use Tretinoin and Azelaic Acid together?
**A:** Yes, many people use Azelaic Acid (like [Aziderm](https://pillvibe.com/product-detail/aziderm-cream)) in the morning and Tretinoin (like [Macret](https://pillvibe.com/product-detail/macret-0-1-gel-20g)) at night. However, introduce them one at a time.

## Q: What should I do if my skin is peeling?
**A:** Peeling is common with retinoids like [Tazret Forte](https://pillvibe.com/product-detail/tazret-forte-cream-for-healthy-skin). Try the "sandwich method" (moisturizer-treatment-moisturizer) and reduce frequency of application.

## Q: Is sunscreen necessary every day?
**A:** Absolutely. Many acne treatments increase sun sensitivity. Failing to use SPF can lead to more hyperpigmentation and skin damage.

## Q: Can I use Ivermectin for regular acne?
**A:** [Ivermectin 1% Cream](https://pillvibe.com/product-detail/ivermectin-1-cream-for-rosacea-treatment) is specifically indicated for the inflammatory lesions of rosacea. For regular acne, retinoids or benzoyl peroxide combinations are usually preferred.
