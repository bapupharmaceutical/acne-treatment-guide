# Comprehensive Guide to Retinoids

Retinoids are derivatives of Vitamin A and are considered the "gold standard" in acne treatment and anti-aging.

## Types of Retinoids

### 1. Tretinoin
The most well-researched retinoid. It helps speed up cell turnover and prevents the formation of comedones. 
-   **Common Formulations:** Available in various strengths (0.025%, 0.05%, 0.1%). [Macret 0.1 Gel](https://pillvibe.com/product-detail/macret-0-1-gel-20g) is a potent option for those who have built tolerance.

### 2. Tazarotene
A third-generation retinoid often used for stubborn acne and psoriasis. It is generally more potent than tretinoin.
-   **Product Highlight:** [Tazret Forte Cream](https://pillvibe.com/product-detail/tazret-forte-cream-for-healthy-skin) is a high-strength option for significant skin improvement.

### 3. Isotretinoin
An oral medication used for severe, cystic acne that hasn't responded to other treatments.
-   **Note:** Requires close medical supervision. [Tretizen 20mg](https://pillvibe.com/product-detail/tretizen-20mg) is a commonly prescribed dosage.

## How to Start Retinoids

-   **Start Slow:** Apply every other night or twice a week initially.
-   **The Sandwich Method:** Apply moisturizer, then retinoid, then moisturizer to reduce irritation.
-   **Sun Protection:** Retinoids increase photosensitivity. Always use SPF during the day.
