# Educational Resources & Further Reading

For those who want to dive deeper into dermatology and skincare science, we recommend the following types of resources:

## Clinical Studies and Journals
-   **Journal of the American Academy of Dermatology (JAAD):** A primary source for clinical research.
-   **PubMed:** Search for specific active ingredients like "Tazarotene" or "Azelaic Acid" to see clinical trial results.

## Reliable Online Information
-   **American Academy of Dermatology (AAD):** Excellent patient education materials on acne and rosacea.
-   **DermNet NZ:** An exhaustive resource for identifying skin conditions with high-quality imagery.
