## Key Considerations

-   **Severity:** Mild acne might only need one active ingredient, while moderate to severe acne often requires a combination.
-   **Tolerance:** Always patch test new products to check for sensitivity.
-   **Synergy:** Some ingredients (like Azelaic Acid and Tretinoin) work well together when introduced carefully.
